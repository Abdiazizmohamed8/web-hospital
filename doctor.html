Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment with a specialist doctor?

You can book an appointment by clicking the "Book Now" button next to each doctor's profile or by visiting our Appointment page. You may also call our hospital directly.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we do accept walk-in patients; however, we recommend scheduling an appointment to reduce waiting time and ensure you see the right specialist promptly.

Are the consultations covered by insurance?

We accept several insurance providers. Please contact our billing department or check our Contact page for more detailed insurance information.

What safety measures are in place during the COVID-19 pandemic?

Badbaado Hospitaal follows all government and WHO guidelines, including mandatory mask-wearing, social distancing, frequent sanitization, and health screening at the entrance.
