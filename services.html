Our Core Medical Services

General Medicine

Our General Medicine department provides comprehensive diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care for adults with a variety of acute and chronic illnesses. From preventive screenings to management of complex conditions, our physicians employ evidence-based approaches tailored to individual patient needs.

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare for newborns, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians are trained to address growth, development, vaccinations, and childhood illnesses, ensuring a safe and nurturing environment for young patients.

Emergency Care

We offer 24/7 emergency medical services equipped with state-of-the-art technology and highly trained emergency physicians. Our rapid response team provides immediate care for trauma, acute illnesses, and life-threatening conditions.

Radiology

Our Radiology department offers advanced diagnostic imaging including digital X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasound. Expert radiologists interpret images accurately to support timely diagnosis and treatment planning.

Surgery

Our surgical team comprises highly skilled surgeons specializing in general, orthopedic, and minimally invasive surgeries. We ensure safe operative procedures and comprehensive postoperative care for optimal patient outcomes.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics, interventions, and rehabilitation. Our cardiologists employ cutting-edge technology for managing heart disease, arrhythmias, and preventive cardiology.

Our Specialized Departments

Oncology

The Oncology department provides holistic cancer care, including early detection, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and supportive care services aimed at improving quality of life.

Pulmonology

Focused on respiratory health, we diagnose and treat lung diseases such as asthma, COPD, pneumonia, and pulmonary fibrosis with advanced respiratory therapy options.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists treat injuries and disorders related to bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles. Services include trauma management, joint replacement, and rehabilitation therapy.

Ophthalmology

Providing comprehensive eye exams, surgical interventions, and corrective treatments to preserve and enhance vision health for patients of all ages.

ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

Our ENT specialists diagnose and treat disorders affecting hearing, balance, sinus, throat, and voice, using both medical and surgical approaches.

Neonatology

Dedicated to the care of premature and critically ill newborns, our neonatology unit provides specialized monitoring, life support, and family-centered care.
