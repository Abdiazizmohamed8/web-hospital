About Badbaado Hospitaal

Established in 2003, Badbaado Hospitaal is one of Somalia's leading hospitals, providing a wide range of services with expert doctors and modern equipment. We prioritize quality, safety, and respect in every patient interaction.

  • 24/7 Emergency & Ambulance
  • Over 67 Experienced Nursing Staff
  • State-of-the-art Medical Equipment
  • Affordable and Accessible Care

Our Vision

To lead in providing world-class, compassionate, and innovative health solutions that enrich lives and strengthen communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the health and wellbeing of our community through excellent patient care, continuous medical education, and community involvement.

Why Choose Us?

  • Trusted for over 20 years
  • Multi-specialty doctors
  • In-house laboratory and pharmacy
  • Prime location in Mogadishu with easy access
  • Community-focused & patient-first policy
